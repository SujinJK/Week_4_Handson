# Nimbus Cloud Storage — Refund Policy

## Standard Refunds

Monthly subscriptions can be canceled at any time; cancellation stops future
billing but does not refund the current billing period. Annual subscriptions
are eligible for a prorated refund if canceled within the first 30 days of
purchase.

## Billing Errors

If a customer is charged incorrectly (duplicate charge, wrong plan tier,
charge after cancellation), Nimbus issues a full refund of the erroneous
charge within 5 business days of the report being verified. Customers should
report billing errors through the "Billing" section of account settings, not
through general support email.

## Downgrades

Downgrading from Business or Pro to a lower tier takes effect at the start of
the next billing cycle. If a downgrade would put a customer over their new
plan's storage limit, Nimbus gives a 14-day grace period to remove files
before access is restricted (not deleted).

## Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds are not issued for: partial months of usage on a canceled monthly
plan, storage overage charges that were already consumed, or accounts
suspended for Terms of Service violations.
