# Nimbus Cloud Storage — Product FAQ

## Storage Plans

Nimbus offers three storage plans: Starter (100GB, $5/month), Pro (2TB,
$15/month), and Business (10TB, $60/month, includes team sharing for up to 25
seats). All plans include automatic file versioning for the last 30 days.

## File Recovery

Deleted files are held in the Trash for 30 days before permanent deletion. To
recover a file, go to Trash and select "Restore." Files deleted from Trash
manually, or after the 30-day window, cannot be recovered by support — Nimbus
does not keep backups beyond this window.

## Sharing and Permissions

Files and folders can be shared via a link (view-only or edit) or by inviting
a specific email address. Business plan accounts can additionally restrict
sharing to only users within the same company domain.

## Offline Access

The Nimbus desktop app supports offline access for files marked "Available
offline." Changes made offline sync automatically once the device reconnects;
if the same file was edited on two devices while offline, Nimbus creates a
conflicted copy rather than overwriting either version.
