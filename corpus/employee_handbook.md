# Nimbus Cloud Storage — Employee Handbook (excerpt)

## Leave Policy

Full-time employees accrue 1.75 days of paid time off (PTO) per month, capped at
21 days per calendar year. PTO must be requested at least 5 business days in
advance through the HR portal, except for sick leave, which can be taken
same-day with a note to your manager.

Unused PTO carries over up to a maximum of 5 days into the next calendar year.
Anything beyond 5 days is forfeited on January 1st unless local law requires
otherwise.

## Remote Work

Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week without prior approval.
Fully remote arrangements require sign-off from both the employee's manager
and HR, and are reviewed every 6 months.

## Probation Period

New hires are on a 90-day probation period. During probation, either the
employee or Nimbus may end employment with 5 business days' notice. After
probation, the standard notice period is 2 weeks.
